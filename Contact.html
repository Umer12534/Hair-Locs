Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused and unopened products. Please contact us with your order number to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. We're working on expanding our shipping options in the future.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package's journey.

Yes! All Hair Locs products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals.
