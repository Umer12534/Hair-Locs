The Hair Locs

Our Story

For over a decade, The Hair Locs has been dedicated to the art and culture of beautiful, healthy locs. We are more than a salon - we are a community.

From Passion to Profession

Founded in 2012 by master loctician Maya Johnson, The Hair Locs began as a small studio with a big vision: to celebrate and elevate the beauty of natural hair through the art of locs. What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a thriving salon with a team of passionate, skilled stylists.

Our journey has been guided by a deep respect for the cultural significance of locs and a commitment to helping each client express their unique identity through their hair. We believe that locs are not just a hairstyle, but a statement of self-love, heritage, and personal power.

Over the years, we've cultivated a warm, welcoming environment where clients become family. Our salon is a safe space for everyone, regardless of where you are on your loc journey.
